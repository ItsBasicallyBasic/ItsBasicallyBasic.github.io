HunGrrr!

18/08/2020

Project Overview

"HunGrrr!" is the latest title that I'm creating for IGB400 to help polish my portfolio. I thought up the game name, along with the idea during the semester break and thought that IGB400 would give me the perfect opportunity to refine that idea into a fully-fledged game that I would release to the public.

I have always thought it would be nice to get myself into the industry as quickly as possible, so this project would act as a final piece to really kickstart my career. In terms of the industry I would like to fit into I always thought it would be nice to work on smaller projects and work my way up the chain, gaining experience and learning from those around me as I go.

After much research into the kind of jobs that fit within my description, I found myself stumbling upon a lot of mobile game companies. I have never made a mobile game before so I thought that was the perfect platform to create this project on. In summary, the skills I wanted to refine during this project are: 

          •   Mobile Platform Development 
          •   Advanced 'swarm-like' player system 
          •   3D Voxel Art 
          •   Player and enemy animations 
          •   Advertisement and release of a finished title
